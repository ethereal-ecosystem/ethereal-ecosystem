Cookie Policy for My-Ehereal.com

Last Updated: March 16, 2025

1. Introduction to Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us improve your browsing experience, understand user behavior, and provide personalized content related to digital trading and market analysis.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies

  • Purpose: Ensure website functionality
  • Examples:
    • Session authentication
    • Security verification
    • Form submission tracking
  • Retention: Up to 30 days
  • Cannot be disabled

2.2 Analytics Cookies

  • Purpose: Understand user interaction and website performance
  • Providers: Google Analytics
  • Data Collected:
    • Page views
    • Time spent on site
    • User navigation patterns
  • Retention: 12 months

2.3 Marketing Cookies

  • Purpose: Personalized advertising and content
  • Providers:
    • Google Ads
    • Facebook Pixel
  • Data Used:
    • Browsing history
    • Interest-based targeting
  • Retention: 6 months

2.4 Preference Cookies

  • Purpose: Remember user settings
  • Examples:
    • Language preferences
    • Dark/light mode selection
    • Saved filters for trading information
  • Retention: 3 months

3. Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party services that may place cookies on your device:

  • Google Analytics
  • Advertising networks
  • Performance monitoring tools

4. User Consent and Management

4.1 Consent Mechanism

  • Explicit opt-in required before non-essential cookies are deployed
  • Granular consent options available
  • Easy withdrawal of consent at any time

4.2 Cookie Management Options

  1. Browser Settings

    • Chrome: Settings > Privacy > Cookies
    • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security
    • Safari: Preferences > Privacy
    • Edge: Settings > Cookies

  2. Third-Party Tools

    • Google Consent Mode
    • OneTrust Cookie Consent Platform

5. User Rights Under GDPR

You have the right to:
